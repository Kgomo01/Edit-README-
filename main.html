About Us:

A user-friendly app which will people detect possible causes of their symptoms to determine underlying conditions and give the user assistance in order to gain a clearer understanding of their health status. This app will also provide the user with access to health professionals to assist with providing health care centers accessible to the user.

Features:

  1. Login information about the symptoms that the user is experiencing
  2. Fullscreen mode while using the Symptom Checker app to provide a clearer view
  3. Include risk factors according to the symptoms listed by the user
  4. Tab for tracking the activity of the user i order to prevent loss of information
  5. Provide a calender where the user can record their symptoms on a daily basis
  6. Include suggestions of self-medication and where it can be purchased; insert a privacy policy which will commit to safe-guarding the information provided by the user

Why choose our app?

  • Symptom checker apps allow users to record their symptoms conveniently on their phones or smart devices and keep record of the progression of their health status.
  • The app includes various modules for medication tracking, dietry suggestions, and monitoring of vital signs including heart rate and blood pressure.
  • Our smart mobile app allows the recored data to be used for the determination of triggers to prevent the occurance of the condition.
